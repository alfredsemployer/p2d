How this is calculated

No black box. Here's the exact model — adjust any assumption above.

  1. Safe cold sending is ~30 emails / mailbox / day. Your monthly volume ÷ sending days ÷ this rate = mailboxes you actually need.
  2. Mailboxes are grouped onto domains (3 per domain by default). Each domain and mailbox carries a real monthly cost most calculators ignore.
  3. For each platform we pick the cheapest plan that actually fits your volume, prospect count, and required number of sending accounts — then add identical infrastructure cost.
  4. The winner is the lowest true all-in monthly cost — not the lowest sticker price.
